Description

Hanımcılar (can be translated as "Ladies' men" or "Men who value their girl/wife over everything") is a themed polaroid photo album project that I developed for my girlfriend at the time as a new year's present. It was originally a static site where I put some of our photos in the form of polaroid photos with text beneath them, hanged on a bulletin board with red pins. I then turned it into a dynamic site with a database and authentication system to make it publicly accessible, more presentable and shareable. Everyone can now create their own albums, upload their photos with texts and dates; and share them with their loved ones, friends, or the world; with different privacy settings and collaboration options.

Technical Information

  • Language(s): TypeScript, JavaScript, CSS
  • Framework(s): React (TSX)
  • Database(s): Firebase - Cloud Firestore & Storage
  • Hosting: Firebase
  Other:

  • APIs - Google Drive Picker
